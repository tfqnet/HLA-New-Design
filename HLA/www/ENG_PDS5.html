Convertible Comprehensive Term Rider This rider does not cover the following occurrences:
i.     Death of the life assured due to suicide during the first 12 months from the date of issuance or reinstatement date, whichever is later.
ii.     Total and Permanent disability resulted directly or indirectly:
       (a)     from any self-inflicted bodily injury while sane or insane;
       (b)     from any nervous disorder or mental illness;
       (c)     from flying in an aircraft (except as an aircrew member of, or as an ordinary fare paying passenger, on a regularly scheduled flight of a
                commercial airline); or
       (d)     from anything whatsoever while as a member of the armed forces, police and paramilitary forces as a result of declared or undeclared war, riots
                or civil commotion.
