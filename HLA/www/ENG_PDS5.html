i.     Death of the life assured due to suicide during the first 12 months from the date of issuance or reinstatement date, whichever is later.
ii.     Total and Permanent disability resulted directly or indirectly:

                or civil commotion.
Critical Illness Rider This rider does not cover the following occurrences:
i.     An episode of coronary artery or ischaemic heart disease that occurred before the Issue Date or any reinstatement date, whichever is later
ii.    Diagnosis of the critical illness other than those specified under item (iii) below within 30 days from the Issue Date or any reinstatement date,
       whichever is later
iii.    Diagnosis of the critical illness specified below within 60 days from the Issue Date or any reinstatement date, whichever is later:
       (a)     Angioplasty and other invasive treatment for coronary artery disease
       (b)     Cancer
       (c)     Coronary artery disease requiring surgery
       (d)     Heart attack
       (e)     Other serious coronary artery disease
iv.    Other than the first incidence of the critical illnesses. The exceptions to this clause are coronary artery disease and AIDS.
v.    Death of the life assured within 28 days following the date of diagnosis of any of the critical illness.

Comprehensive Personal Accident Rider The Company will not pay any benefits under this rider for any loss, damage or other contingency occasioned by or through or in consequence, directly or
indirectly, from:

i.     Flying or taking part in any aerial activities except where traveling in an aircraft as a fare-paying passenger and not as aircrew nor for the purpose of any
       trade or technical operation in or on the aircraft;
ii.    Self-inflicted injury, suicide or attempted suicide, provoked murder or assault or being under the influence of drugs / narcotics / alcohol of any kind;
iii.   Engaging in or taking part in professional or semi-professional sports;
iv.   Engaging in racing of any kind (other than on foot), mountain or rock climbing necessitating the use of ropes or guides, winter sports, underwater
      pastimes, water skiing, football, polo, hunting, show jumping, caving, pot-holing, boxing or wrestling;
v.    War, invasion, act of foreign enemy, hostilities or warlike operations (whether war be declared or not), civil war, military or usurped power;
vi.   Direct participation in strikes, riots, rebellion, revolution, civil commotion or insurrection;
vii.  Active duty in the Armed Forces (whether voluntary or otherwise);
viii. Sickness or disease of any kind caused by or infected by or in any way attributed to virus, parasite, bacteria or any micro-organism including where the
      virus, parasite, bacteria or micro-organism is introduced and/or caused by bites of insects or is sexually transmitted;
ix.   Any medical or surgical treatment (except those necessitated by injuries covered by this Policy);
x.    Committing or attempting to commit any unlawful act;
xi.   Any disease, sickness or congenital defects;
xii.  Any physical or mental defect or infirmity;
xiii. Human Immune-deficiency Virus (HIV) and/or any HIV related illness including AIDS and/or any mutant derivations or variations thereof;
xiv. Pregnancy, childbirth, miscarriage or any complications related to the same;
xv.  Any dental treatment not necessitated by injury covered under this Policy.

Hospitalisation Benefit The said hospitalisation benefit shall not payable:
i.     for more than the first 52 weeks or any longer period during which the Life Assured is continuously confined to hospital
ii.    in respect of the whole of any period of continuous confinement to hospital if any part of such period is within the first 30 days of the Effective Date
      or date of reinstatement of the policy at any time
iii.   confinement to hospital for any illness or injury occurring before payment of the first premium for policy
iv.   confinement to hospital which occurs after the anniversary of the Issue Date next following the attainment by the Life Assured of the age of 60 years
v.    in respect of any period of confinement to hospital which occurs after the Expiry Date
vi.   confinement to hospital for an illness or injury directly or indirectly caused or accelerated by:
       (a)     strike, riot, civil commotion, war (whether declared or not)
       (b)     engaging in aviation (except as an ordinary fare paying passenger on a regularly scheduled flight of a commercial airline)
       (c)     being under the influence of intoxicating liquor or any narcotic or drug
       (d)     attempted suicide, intentional self-injury, dueling, resisting lawful arrest by a lawful authority, or the due process of law
       (e)     insanity
       (f)      venereal disease
       (g)     childbirth, pregnancy, miscarriage and their sequelae
       (h)     the existence of HIV or AIDS Related Complex
