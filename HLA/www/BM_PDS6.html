7. Can I cancel my policy?
Basic Plan
You may cancel the policy by giving a written notice to Hong Leong Assurance Berhad. After the free look period the cash surrender value (if any) of your policy will be paid to you when you cancel the policy, however the amount payable may be less than the total premiums you had paid.
