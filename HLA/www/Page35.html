-     Total & Permanent Disability due to accident - refer to schedule of indemnities
-     Temporary total disability - weekly indemnity of RM8 per 1,000 sum assured
-     Temporary partial disability - weekly indemnity of RM2 per 1,000 sum assured

The Company will not pay any benefits under this rider for any loss, damage or other contingency occasioned by or through or in consequence, directly or indirectly, from:

iii.   Engaging in or taking part in professional or semi-professional sports;
iv.   Engaging in racing of any kind (other than on foot), mountain or rock climbing necessitating the use of ropes or guides, winter sports,         underwater pastimes, water skiing, football, polo, hunting, show jumping, caving, pot-holing, boxing or wrestling;
v.    War, invasion, act of foreign enemy, hostilities or warlike operations (whether war be declared or not), civil war, military or usurped        power;
vi.   Direct participation in strikes, riots, rebellion, revolution, civil commotion or insurrection;
vii.  Active duty in the Armed Forces (whether voluntary or otherwise);
viii. Sickness or disease of any kind caused by or infected by or in any way attributed to virus, parasite, bacteria or any micro-organism        including where the virus, parasite, bacteria or micro-organism is introduced and/or caused by bites of insects or is sexually        transmitted;
ix.   Any medical or surgical treatment (except those necessitated by injuries covered by this Policy);
x.    Committing or attempting to commit any unlawful act;
xi.   Any disease, sickness or congenital defects;
