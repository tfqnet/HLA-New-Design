Description of Basic Plan Benefits

This is a 25-year participating endowment plan with Guaranteed Yearly Income (GYI). The Guaranteed Yearly Income is payable from end of 1st policy year until maturity. The premium payment term of this plan is limited to 6 years only.

Death Benefit
In the event of Death of the Life Assured, the Death benefit equivalent to the sum of the following shall be payable:
(i)    Guaranteed Death Benefit as shown in table below:
Entry Age Guaranteed Death Benefit
0 to 40 100% of outstanding GYI + 100% of Guaranteed Maturity Benefit
41 to 60 Maximum of [(100% of outstanding GYI + 50% of Guaranteed Maturity Benefit), 100% of Guaranteed Maturity Benefit]
(ii)    Accumulated Guaranteed Yearly Income (if any),
(iii)   Accumulated Cash Dividend (if any) and
(iv)   Fund Value (if any), and
(iv)   Special Terminal Dividend (if any)


Total and Permanent Disability Benefit
In the event of TPD (natural causes) (prior to attaining age 65) of the Life Assured, the TPD benefit payable shall be 100% of the Total Premium Paid. In addition, all future premium due thereafter shall be waived. All other benefits shall remain intact after a TPD claim.
Guaranteed Yearly Income
This plan provides Guaranteed Yearly Income of RM<> starting from end of 1st year up to maturity.

Maturity Benefit
The Policy will mature upon survival of the Life Assured at the end of the policy term. A Maturity Benefit equivalent to the sum of the following shall be payable:

(i)    Guaranteed Maturity Benefit as shown in table below:
Entry Age Guaranteed Maturity Benefit
0 to 50 12.5 times of Guaranteed Yearly Income
51 to 60 11 times of Guaranteed Yearly Income
(ii)    Accumulated Guaranteed Yearly Income (if any),
(iii)   Accumulated Cash Dividend (if any)
(iv)   Fund Value (if any), and
(iv)   Special Terminal Dividend (if any)
