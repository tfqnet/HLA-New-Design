1. What is this product about?
- This is a non-participating term rider, which provides continuous cover for all stages of cancer.
2. What are the covers / benefits provided?
The Rider Sum Assured is RM{SumAssured} and duration of coverage is {RiderTerm} years or upon termination, whichever occurs first.

Cancer Benefit
The amount of Benefits as described in the table below shall be payable upon diagnosis of cancer covered of the Life Assured during the coverage period. Upon admission of a claim, all future premiums shall be waived thereafter.
Payment Schedule:
Diagnosis Amount Payable
Very Early Cancers RM {VeryEarlyCancers}
Early Cancers RM {EarlyCancers}
Advanced Cancers RM {AdvancedCancers}
The amount payable in respect of each cancer stage shall be reduced by any prior payments for cancer of same organ or type and shall be limited to 300% of the Rider Sum Assured. For Very Early Cancers and Early Cancers, multiple claims are allowed for the same category stage, subject to that the claim is for a different type of cancer affecting a different organ.
Nursing Care Allowance
Should the Life Assured upon diagnosis by a registered Medical Practitioner to be suffering from Advanced Cancer, the Company shall upon receipt of proof of the said event, pays Policy Owner or his lawful assignee an annual payment equivalent to 25% Rider Sum Assured for a period of 2 years subject to the survival of Life Assured.

Extension of Coverage for Advanced Cancer
A period of 2 Policy Years shall be extended after the Expiry Date to cover Advanced Cancers if there were valid claims of either Very Early Cancers and/or Early Cancers of the same type or the same organ admitted prior to the Expiry Date.
