5. What are some of the key terms and conditions that I should be aware of?
- Importance of disclosure - you must disclose all material facts such as medical condition, and state your age correctly.
- Free-look period - you may cancel your policy by returning the policy within 15 days after you have received the policy. The premiums that you have paid (less any medical fee incurred) will be refunded to you.
- Qualifying / waiting period - the eligibility for benefits under the policy will only start 90 days after the effective date of the policy.
- All applications are subject to underwriting approval.

Note: This list is non-exhaustive. Please refer to the policy contract for the terms and conditions under this policy.
6. What are the major exclusions under this policy?

This rider does not cover the following occurrence:

Cancer Benefit:
- Pre-Existing Condition.
- All Cancers presenting or diagnosed within the Waiting Period of 90 days.
- All Cancers which are histologically classified as being:
- Premalignant (for example cervical intraepithelial / CIN I, II, or III, essential thrombocythemia, polycythemia rubra vera) or having either borderline malignancy / low malignant potential and/or;
- Carcinoma in situ (except Ductal carcinoma in situ breast, Carcinoma in situ cervix and Carcinoma in situ colon).
- All skin Cancers except malignant melanoma with thickness in excess of 1.5 mm thickness or metastatic skin Cancers.
- All Cancers associated with HIV infection.
- Unreasonable failure to follow medical treatment.
- All Cancers if Death of the Life Assured occurs within thirty (30) days following the date of diagnosis of Cancer.

Note: This list is non-exhaustive. Please refer to the policy contract for the full list of exclusions under this policy.
7. Can I cancel my policy?

You may cancel the rider by giving a written notice to Hong Leong Assurance Berhad. After the free look period the cash surrender value (if any) of your rider will be paid to you when you cancel the rider provided that no claim has been admitted prior to cancellation, however the amount payable may be less than the total premiums you had paid.
8. What do I need to do if there are changes to my contact details?

It is important that you inform us of any change in your contact details (including that of the nominee and/or trustee) to ensure that all correspondences reach you and/or nominee/trustee in a timely manner.

Should you require additional information about medical & health insurance, please refer to the insurance info booklet on 'Medical & Health Insurance', available at all our branches or you can obtain a copy from the insurance agent or visit www.insuranceinfo.com.my.
