Riders Sum Assured/Benefit Coverage Period Description of Benefits
Convertible Comprehensive Term Rider {CCTRGYI} {CCTRRiderTerm} RM {CCTRRiderGYI} is payable upon death / TPD (prior to age 65) of the life assured during the coverage term. TPD benefit will be paid in accordance to TPD
provision. During the term of the rider and before attaining age 60, the life assured may convert the policy to a whole life plan or endowment without further
evidence of insurability.
Critical Illness Rider {CIRRiderGYI} {CIRRiderTerm} RM {CIRRiderGYI} is payable upon diagnosis of any of the 36 critical illnesses covered of the life assured during the coverage term.
Premium is not guaranteed and is subject to review by the Company at any time and from time to time by giving you at least 90-day written notice.
The following 36 critical illnesses are covered:

1. Stroke 19. Loss of Speech
2. Heart Attack 20. Brain Surgery
3. End Stage Kidney Failure 21. Heart Valve Surgery
4. Cancer 22. Terminal Illness

7. Angioplasty And Other Invasive Treatments For Major Coronary Artery Disease * 25. Major Head Trauma
8. End Stage Liver Failure 26. Chronic Aplastic Anemia
9. Fulminant Viral Hepatitis 27. Motor Neuron Disease
10. Coma 28. Parkinson's Disease
11. Benign Brain Tumor 29. Alzheimer's Disease/Irreversible Organic Degenerative Brain Disorders
12. Paralysis/Paraplegia 30. Muscular Dystrophy
13. Blindness /Total Loss Of Sight 31. Surgery To Aorta
14. Deafness/Total Loss Of Hearing 32. Multiple Sclerosis
15. Major Burns 33. Primary Pulmonary Arterial Hypertension

17. Encephalitis 35. Severe Cardiomyopathy
18. Major Organ / Bone Marrow Transplant 36. Systemic Lupus Erythematosus With Lupus Nephritis

*Benefit payment under this illness is limited to 10% of the Critical Illness coverage under this plan subject to a maximum of RM 25,000. This benefit is payable

Comprehensive Personal Accident Rider {CPARiderGYI} {CPARiderTerm} The following benefits are payable upon death or disability of the life assured caused by an accident during the coverage period:
-     Total & Permanent Disability due to accident - refer to schedule of indemnities
-     Temporary total disability - weekly indemnity of RM8 per 1,000 sum assured
-     Temporary partial disability - weekly indemnity of RM2 per 1,000 sum assured
-     Medical fees & expenses reimbursement - up to RM20 per 1,000 sum assured
-     Double indemnity - sum assured payable is doubled if accidental death of Life Assured occurred while the Life Assured is on mechanically
      propelled Public Conveyance over an established route on land, excluding Taxis

Extended TPD Rider {ETPDRiderGYI} {ETPDRiderTerm} Upon TPD of the Life Assured during the coverage term and the Life Assured remained disabled, RM {ETPDGYI} shall be payable annually up to age 65 or death of the Life Assured, whichever is earlier.The first payment is on the 1st anniversary subsequent to the commencement of TPD.
Hospitalisation Benefit {HBRiderGYI} {HBRiderTerm} Hospitalisation benefit of RM {HBBenefit} per week is payable if the life assured hospitalized during the coverage period.
Income 20 Rider {I20RRiderGYI} {I20RRiderTerm} Death/TPD Benefit
In the event of Death/TPD (natural causes) (prior to attaining age 65) of the Life Assured, the Death/TPD (natural causes) benefit payable shall be the sum of 100% of outstanding Guaranteed Yearly Income, Accumulated Guaranteed Yearly Income (if any), Accumulated Cash Dividend (if any) and Special Terminal Dividend (if any).

In the event of accidental TPD (prior to attaining age 65) of the Life Assured, the accidental TPD benefit payable shall be the sum of 400% of outstanding Guaranteed Yearly Income, Accumulated Guaranteed Yearly Income (if any), Accumulated Cash Dividend (if any) and Special Terminal Dividend (if any).

TPD benefit will be paid in accordance to TPD provision as illustrated under Descriptions of Basic Plan Benefits.

Guaranteed Yearly Income Benefit
Guaranteed Yearly Income of RM {I20RGYI} is payable starting from end of year 1 up to expiry.

Advancement of Future Guaranteed Yearly Income (GYI)
Policy Owner may advance the next 5 years' GYI upon happening of events as listed below; starting from the rider year where the first GYI is payable and provided that the policy has acquired sufficient Net Cash Surrender Value.

       -     Marriage
       -     Child Birth
       -     Children's university admission
       -     Children's graduation/convocation
       -     Children's wedding
       -     Purchase of new house
       -     Starting of a business
       -     Business Disruption
       -     Policy Owner/ Parents/ Children admitted to hospital
       -     Parent/children death
       -     Policy owner diagnosed with critical illness
       -     Policy owner suffers temporary total disablement due to illness or accident.

The above listed events are not exhaustive and the Company may include more events at its discretion from time to time.

The amount payable to Policy Owner shall be 85% of the total next 5 years' GYI. Thereafter, the GYI due for the next 5 years immediately following the advancement shall cease to be payable in order to offset the said advancement.

Upon surrender or death/ TPD claim, the Company will deduct the outstanding GYI advanced accrued with interest of 5.67% per annum (if any) before making any payment.

Maturity Benefit
Upon maturity at the end of the rider term, a maturity benefit equivalent to the sum of Accumulated Guaranteed Yearly Income (if any), Accumulated Cash Dividend (if any) and Terminal Dividend (if any) shall be payable.
