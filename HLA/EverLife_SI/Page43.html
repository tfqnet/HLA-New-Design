Total Benefit Ð Death/TPD/OAD

Provision for OAD Benefit
Total OAD Benefits per Life payable under all policies insuring the Life Assured shall limit to RM 1,000,000 per life. The Total OAD Benefit per Life refers to OAD coverage of all in force policies for each Life Assured at the point of claim event.
